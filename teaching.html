Teaching

Teaching Approach

Patricia’s teaching integrates mathematical and statistical rigour with reproducible practice and applied problem-solving. She has extensive experience in course design, ranging from large first-year classes to specialist master’s programs. Her approach emphasises inclusive and effective learning environments that connect academic concepts with the skills required in industry and government, preparing students for real-world challenges.

Experience in Leadership in Teaching and Learning: Director of Education, Department of Econometrics and Business Statistics, Monash University

From November 2022 to February 2024, I held a leadership position in teaching and learning within the department, which concluded when I moved to the University of Melbourne.
